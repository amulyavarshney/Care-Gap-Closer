    Google ADK and Gemini pick which MCP tools to call over A2A v1. YAML rules decide the gaps. The model writes the rationale and the patient message.

    Demo mode plays the canned replies from the project demo script. Live mode POSTs message/send with FHIR context in the metadata.

    Who does what

    A YAML rule engine marks overdue care. Gemini writes the clinician rationale and the patient copy for those gaps.

    01

    A2A agent

    Google ADK and Gemini choose tools and carry evidence from one turn to the next.

    02

    MCP tools

    FastMCP reads FHIR R4 through SHARP headers. FHIR tokens stay out of prompts.

    03

    YAML rules

    13 USPSTF and ACIP rules keyed to SNOMED, LOINC, CPT, and CVX codes.

    04

    Gemini authorship

    One-sentence clinical rationale, plus SMS or portal copy at about a sixth-grade reading level. Each rule has a template if Gemini is down.
